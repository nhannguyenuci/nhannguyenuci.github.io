Trading/Betting Platform

About the Project

This project will design a user interface for trading assets—such as cryptocurrencies, sports players, or music streams—linked to real-world performance. It will emphasize an intuitive, engaging, and educational experience that highlights real-time data's impact on market behavior.

Problem

Interest is rising in platforms that blend trading, gaming, and real-world data, but few offer a fun, engaging experience. Traditional trading platforms dominate the market, while fantasy sports and predictive games often lack the depth and excitement of real-world trading dynamics.

Solution

The goal is to identify what kind of product would attract users to buy, sell, and trade and to develop a platform that offers a unique, immersive experience for fans of trading, gaming, or both.
